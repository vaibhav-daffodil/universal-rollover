ADMISSION WITHDRAWAL POLICY
  1. Admission fee / Registration fees once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances or for any reason whatsoever.
  2. If a parent / guardian withdraws admission of his / her child / ward at any time before the scheduled commencement of academic term, refund, if any, of fees, will be subject to the following provisions.
    • If an admission is withdrawn on / or before 30 days prior to the commencement of the academic year, full tuition fee and term fee will be refunded.
    • If an admission is withdrawn by giving less than 30 days notice prior to the scheduled date of commencement of the academic term, one month tuition fees will be payable by the parent / guardian to the school.
  3. After the commencement of the academic term if a child / ward does not report to school at all and if the admission is withdrawn at any point of time, one month tuition fee and one term fee will be payable by the parent / guardian to the school.
  4. After the commencement of the academic term, if a child / ward attends the school even for a single day & if the admission is withdrawn, the following fees will be payable by the parent / guardian to the school.
    1. Fee till the month of withdrawal application calculated from the month of the commencement of the academic term (both months inclusive)
    2. 1 month notice period fee
      1. 1 term fee (in case the admission is withdrawn in the 1st term)
      2. 2 term fees (in case the admission is withdrawn in the 2nd term)
